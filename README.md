# 🌍 Disaster Preparedness & Planning Management

## 📌 Overview
This project presents structured and well-organized study material for the subject **Disaster Preparedness & Planning Management (B22CE56)**. It is designed as a clean, responsive web-based resource covering all major concepts, case studies, and modern techniques used in disaster management.

---

## 🎓 Course Details
- **College:** Vaagdevi College of Engineering (Autonomous)  
- **Branch:** B.Tech – Civil Engineering  
- **Regulation:** R22  
- **Course Code:** B22CE56  
- **Type:** Open Elective  
- **Credits:** 3 (L:3 T:0 P:0)

---

## 📖 Course Objectives
- To understand the state-of-the-art disaster management practices.
- To study various natural disasters and mitigation measures.
- To learn about human-induced hazards and case studies.
- To gain knowledge on Remote Sensing and GIS.
- To explore modern technologies used in disaster management.

---

## 📚 Syllabus

### 🔹 UNIT – I: Introduction
- Hazard, vulnerability and risk  
- Types of disasters  
- Disaster management cycle  
- Role of civil engineers in disaster management  
- Progress of disaster management in the world  
- Vulnerability profile of India  
- Disaster management in India  

---

### 🔹 UNIT – II: Natural Disasters
- Hydro-meteorological disasters:
  - Tropical cyclones  
  - Floods  
  - Drought  
  - Desertification  
- Geographical disasters:
  - Earthquakes  
  - Tsunamis  
  - Landslides  
  - Avalanches  
- Coastal zone management  

---

### 🔹 UNIT – III: Human Induced Hazards
- Chemical industrial hazards  
- Major power breakdowns  
- Traffic accidents  
- Case studies  

---

### 🔹 UNIT – IV: Remote Sensing & GIS
- Introduction to Remote Sensing  
- Introduction to GIS  
- Applications in disaster mitigation and management  
- Case studies  

---

### 🔹 UNIT – V: Disaster Management
- Risk assessment  
- Hazard mapping  
- Mitigation and management options  
- Warning and forecasting systems
